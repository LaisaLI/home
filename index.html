Research Interests

Over the past year, I have focused on exploring and researching LLMs, particularly their role as database interfaces. Through my research and observations, I have identified several challenges: the inherent ambiguity of natural language versus the need for precise answers, and the generalized knowledge of LLMs compared to the specific domain knowledge required for different databases.

I am currently concentrating on developing methods to automatically generate domain knowledge and contextual information to bridge this gap effectively.
