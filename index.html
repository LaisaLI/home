About Me

I'm a passionate researcher focusing on Large Language Models (LLMs) and their applications. My interest in these technologies has driven me to explore various research projects, aiming to contribute meaningful advancements in the field. I am currently preparing to pursue a Ph.D. program at The University of Hong Kong, starting in Fall 2025.

Research Interests

In the past year, I have focused on understanding LLMs, particularly their use as interactive database interface systems to design large SQL queries. My recent submissions include work on LLMs related to scheduling challenges. Additionally, my experience as a data scientist has brought me solid skills in statistics, machine learning, and mathematical optimization.
