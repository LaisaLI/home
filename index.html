Research Interests

Over the past year, I have focused on exploring LLMs, particularly their potential as database interfaces. My research has revealed several challenges, including the inherent ambiguity of natural language contrasted with the need for precise answers, as well as the general knowledge of LLMs versus the specific domain expertise required for different databases. This underscores the necessity for strong reasoning capabilities within LLMs.

I am currently investigating how to build a high-quality dataset derived from industry use cases, along with the potential to automatically generate data samples and domain knowledge for each specific domain.
